Small Groups

Want a group of brothers or sisters you can do life with? Small Groups are a great opportunity for a student to have a community and know there are other guys or girls to help them get through the semester with God.

We have gender-based small groups, which means separate guys and girls small groups so that individuals can feel free to share their struggles+advice+prayers that may be specific to that gender. Each small group is assigned with a leader or co-leader. We officially call our leaders “Soon-jangs”, and call our small group members “Soon-wons”. These titles are direct Korean translations of leader and disciple.

If you are interested in joining a small group reach out to one of the leaders or attend one of our large group meetings!
