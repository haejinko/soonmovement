Higher Calling

Higher Calling is a yearly conference where regional Soon Movement campus ministries come together to worship, pray, and have fellowship with one another. As a student you will be placed in a small group with other students from different campuses and you will walk through the conference with them. The conference invites great pastors and speakers to come share the message and give seminars specific for college students. This conference is a great opportunity for students to encounter God and learn wisdom on how to grow in their faith on campus and for their future with the Lord.

Missions

Summer Missions is a yearly mission trip, where we send teams from each Soon Movement region of America to share the Gospel. We send them to prospective countries in Europe, Asia, and areas in America as well to go witness to other college students or people of the nation in that area. Summer missions a great way to really get out of our comfort zones and experience how God is moving in other cultures and nations.
